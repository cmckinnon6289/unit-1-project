Government of Lorem - News Release

Statement from the Prime Minister of Lorem

29th of September, 2023

It has become apparent to me that I no longer have the confidence of Parliament. I understand that many of my consituents and colleagues are upset with my recent decisions. It is my duty to lead my government the way I see fit, but only as a representative of the wishes of the people.

This is not a concession of politics; I maintain my stances on all political issues. I will, however, apologize for not being representative of the people of Lorem. This is one of the biggest principles of our democracy.

As a result, I will be going to His Majesty the King tonight to request a general election be held before year's end. It has been a pleasure to serve you all as Prime Minister of Lorem.
