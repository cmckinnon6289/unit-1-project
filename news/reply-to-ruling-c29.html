Government of Lorem - News Release

Government statement in response to Supreme Court ruling on Bill C-29

17th of September, 2023

The Supreme Court of Lorem has struck down Bill C-29. In a unanimous ruling, the court held that the bill was unconstitutional on the grounds of "unreasonable search and seizure" of private property.

We are committed to providing our citizens liberty and protection, as well as maintaining the delicate balance between the two. The government will reconvene and draft this bill again to ensure it is in full compliance with human rights legislation and the Constitution.
