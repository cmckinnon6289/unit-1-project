Government of Lorem - News Release

General election to be held

30th of September, 2023

At the request of the Prime Minister of Lorem, His Majesty the King announced earlier today that a general election shall take place on the 5th of November, 2023.

All Loremians who are 18 years of age or older are eligible to vote. Voting sites will be setup across the country in the coming days. All voters need on the day is a piece of photo ID with their name and address on it.

Lorem Elections is hiring. Interested individuals should contact the Chief Electoral Officer with a resume.
