Open letter to the Prime Minister of Lorem

19th of September, 2023

Dear Prime Minister Ipsum,

I no longer feel comfortable serving under your leadership. It has become apparent that you do not represent the people of Lorem, only seeking to line the pockets of your rich donors. Your colleagues may be comfortable with such arrangements, but I am not, and clearly my fellow ministers agree with me.

The constitution needs to be written. Until then, I refuse to work with you. You have lost the confidence of the House and must call an election immediately. Anything else you do as Prime Minister is a blatant subversion of the sacred institution of democracy that I tried to uphold, despite your tyranny. Please accept my resignation from the role of Minister of Finance.

Sincerely,

Vivienne Abhilasha
