Purpose of the Justice System

The justice system of Lorem is designed to provide a fair, unbiased adjudication of any legal dispute that may rise, and to uphold the principles of fundamental justice established long before us. Lorem's courts and legal staff work diligently to enforce and uphold the laws and constitution, ensuring they are reflective of our strong societal values.
